Chapter I

Born from a real manuscript

Before there were apps, there was an engine. JumashEmet began as the pipeline that carried 1,186 WhatsApp voice notes — years of spoken Torah — into a complete, chronological manuscript. Not a demo, a finished body of work.

The Torah apps are that same conviction, turned outward: that learning deserves structure, that questions deserve a place to live, and that what is spoken should not be lost. Each app is one door into the same house of study.

— The founding work of the JumashEmet engine

Chapter III

The Truth of Where It Stands

Deployed · Honest Status

The suite is built and deployed inside the Pinjas app at app.pinjasemet.com — real code, real database migrations, live on our own infrastructure, with genuine per-account usage limits enforced server-side and verified against the running deployment.

One honest caveat: the AI-generation features (roadmap drafting, generated answers) are wired end-to-end but await API-key activation. Until those keys are installed they return a clear error — not a fake demo, and not a promise dressed up as a product. Everything else works today.
