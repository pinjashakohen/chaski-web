The Pipeline

From raw audio to finished manuscript

A two-layer engine. L1 captures everything faithfully. L2 shapes it into something you'd be proud to publish.

01 · INGEST

Drop your export

Upload a WhatsApp chat zip. Audio, timestamps, and senders are parsed automatically — nothing manual.

02 · L1 ARCHIVE

Verbatim transcript

ASR transcribes every clip into a chronological archive, exported as Markdown and PDF — preserving who said what, and when.

03 · L2 MANUSCRIPT

Polished document

Thematic sections, borrowed-word titles, elegant typography. A readable manuscript — never paraphrased, always faithful.

04 · DELIVER

Wherever you are

Sent back to WhatsApp, email, or your own CHASKY space. Ready to share, archive, or register for copyright.
